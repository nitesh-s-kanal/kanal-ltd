FAQ

Common questions

Everything you need to know before working with us.

Still have questions? →
Are you an authorised Zoho Partner in the UK?
+
Yes — Kanal LTD is an officially authorised Zoho Partner serving UK and Ireland businesses. Our engineers hold Zoho certifications across CRM, Books, People, Inventory, and more. We can access Zoho partner pricing and priority support channels on your behalf.
How long does a Zoho implementation typically take?
+
It depends on scope. A Zoho Bigin or single-module setup can be complete in 1–2 weeks. A full Zoho One implementation across CRM, Books, People, and Inventory typically takes 6–12 weeks. We provide a detailed project timeline during discovery — with no nasty surprises.
Can you migrate our data from our current system?
+
Absolutely. We handle data migration from Excel, Sage, QuickBooks, Xero, Salesforce, Dynamics, and most other systems. We clean, map, and validate your data before migration — so you go live with clean, accurate records.
Do you support TallyPrime for UK businesses?
+
Yes. We implement, configure, and support TallyPrime for UK and Ireland businesses, including HMRC Making Tax Digital compliance, multi-company setups, payroll, and inventory. We also build custom TallyPrime-to-Zoho integrations for businesses that use both.
Can you integrate Zoho with our Shopify / WooCommerce / website?
+
Yes. Our API integration team connects Zoho to virtually any platform — Shopify, WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento, Stripe, GoCardless, Royal Mail, DPD, and many more. We build the integration, test it, and maintain it as part of your support retainer.
What kind of ongoing support do you offer post go-live?
+
We offer flexible retainer plans starting from a few hours per month up to dedicated engineer support. Services include bug fixes, feature enhancements, user additions, report building, integration updates, and periodic system health reviews. We're your long-term digital transformation partner.
Do you work with businesses outside Brighton?
+
Absolutely. While our headquarters are in Brighton, we work with clients across the entire UK and Ireland — fully remotely. We use video calls for discovery and training, and can travel to client sites for larger projects if required.
